SRK talks about his son's MMS, terrorism, Ra.One's failure, midlife crisis, his early struggles and more in his TED talk

Shah Rukh Khan delivers an excellent speech on how humanity's future is a reflection of his life as he talks about his struggles and adventures.

Shah Rukh Khan began his first ever TED talk in Vancouver with "Namaskar, I am a movie star, I'm 51 years of age and I don't use Botox as yet."

"I sell dreams, and I peddle love to millions of people."

“I’ve also been made to understand that there are lots of you who haven’t seen my work and I feel very sad for you,” jokes SRK.

In his charming persona, Shah Rukh Khan talks about how internet (especially twitter) changed his life, how he got to meet Angelina Jolie for only 2 seconds, how he dealt with losing both his parents, how his attempt on creating a superhero film failed, how SRK's own baby was claimed on the internet to be his elder son's love child which was alongside a fake MMS video and how he and his family dealt with it.
